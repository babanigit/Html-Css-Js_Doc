What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results.

SEO consists of on-page SEO, off-page SEO, and technical SEO.

On-Page SEO

On-page SEO includes optimizing title tags, meta descriptions, and header tags.

Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO focuses on backlinks, social media signals, and guest blogging.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO includes improving site speed, mobile-friendliness, and structured data.
